# Privacy Policy – SPPTI Extension

SPPTI (Smart Phishing Protection & Threat Intelligence) respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect
- The extension may access the current tab URL to analyze for phishing threats.
- No personal data (name, email, passwords) is collected.

## How We Use Information
- URLs are sent securely to our backend server for analysis.
- The purpose is only to detect phishing or malicious content.

## Data Storage
- We do not store personal user data.
- Scan results may be stored for improving detection performance.

## Third-Party Services
- The extension may use backend APIs for AI-based analysis.

## Security
- All data is transmitted securely.
