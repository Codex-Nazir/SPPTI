What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, banking details, or personal data.

How Phishing Works

  • Fake websites that look real
  • Emails asking to “verify account”
  • Messages with malicious links

Types of Phishing Attacks

  • Email phishing
  • SMS phishing (smishing)
  • Fake login pages
  • Social media scams

Real-Life Example

A common phishing attack involves fake banking emails asking users to verify their account details. These emails contain links that redirect users to fake websites where their login credentials are stolen.

How to Stay Safe

  • Always check the website URL
  • Never click unknown links
  • Use tools like SPPTI to scan links
