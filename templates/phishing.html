What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, banking details, or personal data.

How Phishing Works

  • Fake websites that look real
  • Emails asking to “verify account”
  • Messages with malicious links

How to Stay Safe

  • Always check HTTPS
  • Avoid clicking unknown links
  • Use tools like SPPTI to scan URLs
